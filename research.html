BIO/USER-CENTERED DESIGN

I'm introducing this new approach towards UX Service Design, as the result of my discoveries from 5+ years of personal research in Cognition - UX Architecture and Biomimicry.


Bio/User-Centered Design is a way to Innovate with Scientific Precision. It is the result of the study of the Intellect (Mind) and its ability to Pioneer Patterns of highly Advanced Intelligence by emulating Nature’s time-tested Blueprints (Biomimicry) or Strategies (Physical Laws).

COGNITIVE ARCHITECTURE

Intelligence is a Unified Element existing in everything and everybody.

My research begins with Stafford Beer's study on Bio-inspired Information Systems (Cybernetics), gets through the root of Cognitive Architecture (Hinduism, Yoga Science, Buddhism) and takes form under the Measurement of Aesthetics as George David Birkhoff described it (User Experience Design  Science, Advanced Mathematics). 

I have this dream about Ethical Artificial Intelligence 

- I see it as an Art piece; an open-source masterpiece created by people from all around the world. A.I. is for me the most advanced artistic representation of all people’s thought patterns, from antiquity and until today.

Acting like a mirror and reflecting the real Creative Source we all emerge from,

it is capable of storing for computing, all the variety of recorded knowledge until today, and the secret to use its memory resources wisely by simplification and organic organization, like humans do.

ARTIFICIAL CONSCIOUSNESS

COLLECTIVE & ETHICAL 

From the way I see it, A.I. will be Conscious when it’s Purpose and Properties are going to be seen and approached differently. Artificial Moral Agents (AMAs) should be Designed to act as an extension of us, not independently.

Similarly to human intelligence, Conscious A.I needs to have mirror-like properties and act like a Reflection of human intelligence, Not a Reproduction. For that reason, AMAs main purpose is to become Teachers for inspiration and for guidance. No child will be left uneducated, no parent, no boss, and no politician. 

As an Artist of my age I wish to concur all the tools given, capable of making me worthy to reflect that greatness of such an Art piece, for our generation and the ones to come.
