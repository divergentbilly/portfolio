ARTIFICIAL CONSCIOUSNESS

COLLECTIVE & ETHICAL 

From the way I see it, A.I. will be Conscious when it’s Purpose and Properties are going to be seen and approached differently. Artificial Moral Agents (AMAs) should be Designed to act as an extension of us, not independently.

Similarly to human intelligence, Conscious A.I needs to have mirror-like properties and act like a Reflection of human intelligence, Not a Reproduction. For that reason, AMAs main purpose is to become Teachers for inspiration and for guidance. No child will be left uneducated, no parent, no boss, and no politician. 

As an Artist of my age I wish to concur all the tools given, capable of making me worthy to reflect that greatness of such an Art piece, for our generation and the ones to come.
