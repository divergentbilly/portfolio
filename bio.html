Bio

How it all began ...

Vasileios, born in Athens, Greece and began his studies in Art since a very early age. In 2007 he obtained a BA Honors of Arts in Industrial Product Design from Middlesex University of London.

After around 12 years of professional experience, he have become a millenial Practical Philosopher and Certified Product/Service Designer; a true Guru in what constitutes Human Experiences and Expressions.

I am happy to be able to Coach and Teach Design Thinking and Effective Altruism to all kinds of professionals (Developers, Designers, Marketers, Entrepreneurs, etc.), something which put me on the place of the conductor who orchestrates teams and individuals on cultivating the best version of themselves. Also by being on the wheel of Business Intelligence when I began my own startup some years ago made me see and process assignments like Operations Research, Business, and Financial Planning with great success. The plans I did received seed funding and have been awarded for their innovative approaches.


Although Imagination has been my most strong skill, I've seen and practiced a very wide spectrum of frameworks and types of workflow, which helps me not only see the future while I Design, but also bring it down to a User-Friendly and practically fruitful implementation. Hence, Human Centered Design has been at the core of my Design Thinking methodology.

Lifelong studies in Art & Design, along with my BA in Industrial Product Design drove me across the fundamentals of peoples' needs and behaviors. My intuitive curiosity guides my constant pursuit for meaningful Inquiry on what makes sense and what comes together more naturally. As a result, I don't just have a complete and very thorough skillset on the process of User Engineering which has been my practical experience for the past years (User Research, Product Strategy, Hybrid Design), but I never stopped studying a bunch of other subjects through independent courses and workshops (Psychology, Phenomenology, Ontology, Epistemology, Physics & Biomechanics, Ergonomics, Reasoning & Cognition, Gamification, Yoga Cognition, and many more.). 

THE PLAN

Billy’s plan is to educate people from a diverse knowledge background, help them express their creativity and guide them to find solutions to global problems collaboratively. By this, he aims to bring Synthetic Design as a way to innovate with Scientific Precision, to the leaders of tomorrow, and their machines.
